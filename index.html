Some of our features that stand out

360° student profiles

To overcome challenges in future students these days need an adaptive set of skills. We consider a student’s overall development encompassing academics, extracurriculars, health etc and present proper data to teachers and parents to make their decisions.

CBSE, IGCSE compliancy

Compliancy procedures are automated with the respective boards to save time and money. We ensure that teachers record their statutory forms and draft their weekly plans and daily logs.

Toolset for teachers

Provide teachers the tools, the technology they need which will help them to organize assignments, provide better collaborations from both sides and effective communication.

Strategic transformation

Strategic ideas to transform learning and teaching experiences. Focusing more on quality education to make them prepared for the future.

Transforming school education with modern technology

Understanding 360° of a student will result in better personalization and success. We profile psychometry, academics, health, nutrition, family situation and present actionable data to teachers, counsellors & parents.
