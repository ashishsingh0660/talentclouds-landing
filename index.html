For Schools, Teachers & Parents

TalentClouds is a complete integrated academics, administration and communication management solution for schools helping teachers, students and parents.

Students can easily get all their information and communicate with teachers as well monitor their performance for improvements.

Teachers can organise her daily essential tasks and paperwork as well as monitor student's activities and report to the parents directly.

Parents can monitor their child's activities and take important decisions later. They can talk to the teachers and get updated.

Schools can manage their teachers and other paperwork easily without getting into too much hassle.

Institutes can target their bright students and also help in performance improvement by managing students and faculty

Preschools can take care of the toddlers and keep track of essential information and keep in sync with their parents.
